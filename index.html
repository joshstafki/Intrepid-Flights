About Intrepid Flights

We are passionate storytellers using the latest drone technology to capture unique and compelling aerial visuals. Based in central MN, we serve clients across various industries, bringing visions to life from a bird's-eye view.

Our mission is to provide high-quality, safe, and innovative drone services, ensuring every shot perfectly aligns with your creative or commercial goals. We are fully licensed with the FAA, prioritizing safety and professionalism on every flight.
